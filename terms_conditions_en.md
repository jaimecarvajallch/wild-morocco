## 2. PROGRAM OBJECT AND NATURE

The **Wild Morocco: Extreme Survival Expedition** program is a **structured academic training program** in survival techniques, bushcraft, and community resilience, taught under AEANSB statutes. **Program Character:** Professional certified training. Participants who complete all academic requirements will receive an **Official International Diploma**, accrediting them as a Survival and Bushcraft Monitor according to AEANSB standards.
